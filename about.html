About Us

Logistics experts at Fox Relay specialize in providing last mile delivery services to carriers, solving common and unexpected problems during the final leg all while exceeding consumer expectations.

ased in San Francisco, California, Fox Relay teams up with carriers to complete the delivery cycle no matter the circumstances. Our dedicated team offers the utmost in reliability, professionalism, and ingenuity, ensuring packages make it to consumers in pristine condition and in a timely manner.

We also understand that the experience and satisfaction of your customer and the consumer you’re delivering to is of utmost importance to you, and directly impacts your reputation as well as your bottom line. We treat each package, and each customer we deliver to, as if it were our own, consistently excelling in service and care.
