MODERN FINAL MILE SOLUTIONS

Fox Relay works exclusively with carriers to provide last mile services, handling off-hour deliveries, completing previously failed deliveries, and making fast shipping more cost effective

What Is Fox Relay?

Fox Relay specializes in taking over the last leg of delivery for carriers, moving your customers’ products from the delivery hub directly to the consumer, no matter the time or day.

Today’s consumers demand fast order fulfillments, and tracked packages have become the norm. But few things frustrate them more than seeing that their package is in town on a Friday evening, but won’t be in hand until the next week. Factor in consumer demand for same- or next-day arrival and dealing with multiple retries, and there are several points during the final leg that make or break customer and consumer relations.

With Fox Relay, carriers have more options available to meet consumer demands for quickness and excellence. Our experienced logistic experts provide on-time deliveries, unparalleled protection of products, and the same level of customer care you expect from your own team, ensuring consumers’ have the delivery experience they mandate.

What We Do

Gets Product Delivered Faster

Fox Relay operates on demand, getting packages to their destination even if the delivery time is outside of your fleet’s normal hours of operation.

Complete difficult deliveries

Our last mile solutions reduce cost and eliminate headaches caused by multiple failed deliveries. We make sure packages get in the consumers’ hands, no matter how many tries it takes.

Expand your capabilities at a lower cost

Minimize the number of seasonal drivers needed during peak shipping times while still maintaining and even improving delivery performance.

Tackle heavy packages

Size and weight won’t slow us down – our team is ready to take on those hard-to-deliver packages when you don’t have the manpower to do so.

Keep tracking intuitive

Our GPS tracking system ties in to yours, ensuring consumers always have updated information with just one code.

Provide professional, flexible solutions

We work with you to provide the service consumers and businesses expect, even in unique circumstances. Our experienced team is committed to providing the solutions carriers need to maintain and improve delivery capabilities.
